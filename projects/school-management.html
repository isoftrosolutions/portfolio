School Management System

A comprehensive school management platform built for educational institutions in Nepal. Handles student records, attendance, exams, grades, timetables, and parent-teacher communication in one unified system.

Client: iSoftro Solutions

Project Overview

The School Management System is an all-in-one platform designed to digitize and streamline the daily operations of educational institutions across Nepal. Built with a focus on the local education system, it replaces fragmented paper-based processes with a centralized digital solution that serves administrators, teachers, students, and parents alike.

From enrollment and attendance tracking to exam management and report card generation, every module is designed to reduce administrative overhead and improve communication between the school and families.

Key Modules

Student Management

Enrollment, personal records, document management, class assignment, and academic history tracking for every student.

Attendance System

Daily attendance tracking with per-class and per-student reports, absence notifications, and attendance analytics.

Exam & Grades

Exam scheduling, grade entry, mark sheets, rank calculation, and progress reports for periodic assessments.

Fee Management

Fee structure setup, payment collection, due tracking, receipt generation, and customizable fee categories.

Timetable

Class timetable generation, teacher assignment, room allocation, and schedule conflict detection.

Reports

Report card generation, attendance summaries, fee ledgers, academic performance analytics, and exportable reports.

Communication

Parent-teacher messaging, notice board, circular distribution, and event announcements to keep parents informed.

User Roles

Role-based access for admin, teachers, students, and parents with customized dashboards and permissions.

Tech Stack

PHP & MySQL Bootstrap JavaScript
